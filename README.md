Big Mart Sales Prediction
This project is a regression-based machine learning solution designed to predict the future sales of a global grocery store and supermarket chain. By leveraging data-driven insights, this predictive model helps the company anticipate sales volumes across its various retail locations worldwide.


📌 Problem Statement
A multinational grocery store and supermarket company operates several retail outlets globally. To optimize supply chains, manage inventory, and drive strategic growth, they need an accurate system to forecast future sales.

Why Predict Sales?
Predicting sales revenue yields valuable operational and strategic applications for the business, including:
Predict sales revenue: Generate precise financial projections.
Perceive challenges early: Spot downward trends or performance drops ahead of time to take corrective action.
Aid future marketing plans: Tailor promotions and marketing campaigns to locations with high potential or those needing a boost.
Plan recruitment strategy: Align staffing levels and workforce hiring with peak and off-peak sales periods.
Motivate sales teams: Set realistic, data-backed targets to drive team morale and productivity.
